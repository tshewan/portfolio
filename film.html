Aim

To develop an online platform that engages and connects diverse groups of people that share common interests and passions.

Objectives

  • Engage and Connect: Develop an online platform that engages and connects diverse groups of people sharing common interests and passions.
  • Encourage Positive Communication: Encourage pleasant and positive communication between fans.
  • Connection Opportunities: Provide greater opportunities for artists and fans to develop a connection with each other via the website.
  • Fan Unity: Allow fans to come together and show their love and appreciation for their favorite artists, movies, etc.

Pain Points

Content Discoverability

Users may struggle to find new and relevant content due to poor discoverability features, leading to a stagnant viewing experience.

Social Integration

Lack of social integration can limit engagement, as users prefer platforms that allow easy sharing and discussion of content with friends.

Spoiler Management

Inadequate spoiler controls can ruin user experience by exposing critical plot details unintentionally, detracting from the enjoyment of discovery.

Streaming Quality Issues

Fluctuating streaming quality and buffering issues can significantly impair user satisfaction, especially for those with unstable internet connections.

Implementing regular interactive events can boost user participation and keep the community active and engaged.

Robust communication tools tailored with moderation features are essential for maintaining a positive community environment.

Exclusive content and direct interaction opportunities with artists can enhance the value of the platform to fans.
