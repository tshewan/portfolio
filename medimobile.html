Order and track medicine

Aim

To develop a user-friendly mobile application that enables doctors and pharmacies to efficiently order, track, and manage medicine inventory, improving accessibility to medical supplies and streamlining the medication ordering process.

Objectives

  • Streamlined Ordering and Tracking: Enable doctors and pharmacies to easily place and track medicine orders in real-time.
  • Inventory Management: Assist pharmacies in managing stock levels and reordering supplies to prevent shortages.
  • Notification System: Provide timely updates on order status and stock availability through notifications.
  • Data Security: Ensure secure handling of sensitive information with encryption and access control.

Pain Points

1. Complex Interface

Medical inventory systems are often complex, leading to user errors and inefficiencies in order processing and inventory management.

2. Integration with Existing Systems

Pharmacies and medical facilities use a variety of systems to manage patient records, billing, and inventory, requiring seamless integration with new applications.

3. Real-Time Inventory Tracking

Inaccurate inventory data can lead to critical shortages or excess, disrupting patient care and increasing costs.

4. Security Concerns

Handling sensitive information, including patient data and pharmaceutical details, requires robust security measures to prevent data breaches and ensure compliance with legal standards like HIPAA.

Takeaways

MediMobile: Streamlining Medicine Orders

During the design process, we discovered that simplifying the user interface could significantly reduce order processing times and errors. By implementing a predictive search and real-time tracking features, we improved the efficiency of the ordering process and user satisfaction.

PharmaTrack: Real-time Inventory Management

One key realization was that pharmacies often face challenges in predicting demand for medicines, which can lead to either surplus or shortage. By implementing machine learning algorithms for demand forecasting, PharmaTrack enabled pharmacies to reduce stock discrepancies by up to 30%.
